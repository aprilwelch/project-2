Home

Welcome! Thank you for visiting my website. Here you will find out a little more about me, my interests, my work and my school. I believe that life is about constant expansion. Life should just flow and feel right. Follow your impulses and be a witness to the unfolding of your life. Be brave and expect the best to come to you in any and every moment. You really can be the person you dream you can be. You can do all the things that you want to do. Be fearless enough to selfishly stay true to your dreams as each and everyone of them comes true! The journey is not the reward the real reward is how you feel along the way and that, is your choice not matter what your circumstance. Amazing things, and wonderful experiences are always there for you in your now and in your future. Many people go astray becasue they decide they are going to wait until all their ducks are in a row before they decide its okay to be happy. I suggest you decide to be happy at the first fleeting glimpse of a single duck feather, then just trust that the ducks will show up and line up in their own time. I always say trust the timing of the universe.
